Detailed Description

Driver for the PPZUAV IMU.

Interface and defaults for the PPZUAV IMU.

9DoM IMU with ITG-3200, ADXL345 and HMC5843, all via I2C.

#define IMU_ACCEL_X_SENS   37.91
